# Privacy Policy

**Telugu Chronological Bible**

**Last Updated:** June 24, 2026

---

## **Introduction**

Telugu Chronological Bible (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information and the practices we follow regarding data collection, use, and protection.

This Privacy Policy applies to the App and all related services. By downloading and using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

### **1.1 Personal Information**

Telugu Chronological Bible **does NOT collect any personally identifiable information** from its users. This includes:

- ❌ Your name
- ❌ Email address
- ❌ Phone number
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ IP address
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Age or date of birth
- ❌ Gender or other demographic information

### **1.2 Usage Data**

The App **does NOT collect any usage analytics or tracking data**. This includes:

- ❌ How long you use the App
- ❌ Which chapters or verses you read
- ❌ When you use the App
- ❌ Device information (hardware model, OS version, etc.)
- ❌ Crash or error reports with personal data
- ❌ User behavior or interaction data

### **1.3 Stored Data**

The App stores **only the following on your device** using Android's SharedPreferences:

1. **Last Read Chapter**: The sequence number (1-1420) of the last chapter you read. This allows the App to restore your reading position when you reopen it.
2. **Theme Selection**: Your preference for the display theme (Light, Sepia, or Dark).

**This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted or shared.**

---

## **2. How We Use Information**

Since we do not collect personal information, we have no use for such data. The limited data stored locally (last read chapter and theme preference) is used solely to:

- Improve your user experience by remembering your reading position
- Respect your theme preference

**No data is sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.**

---

## **3. Data Storage & Security**

### **3.1 Local Storage**

All stored data (last read chapter and theme selection) is stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences. This data:

- ✓ Never leaves your device
- ✓ Is not backed up to cloud services
- ✓ Is not synced with servers
- ✓ Is not shared with third parties
- ✓ Is only accessible to the App

### **3.2 Data Deletion**

If you uninstall the App, all stored data is automatically deleted from your device. You can also clear the App's data at any time through your device's Settings:

**Android → Settings → Apps → Telugu Chronological Bible → Storage → Clear Data**

### **3.3 Security Measures**

We implement the following security measures:

- ✓ Code obfuscation to prevent reverse engineering
- ✓ Asset encryption for Bible text files
- ✓ No transmission of data over networks
- ✓ No third-party SDKs or libraries that collect data

## **6. Children's Privacy**

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is suitable for readers of all ages and contains religious educational content (the Bible). Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise children's use of the App.

We do not have any special mechanisms for collecting data from children because we do not collect data from anyone.

---

## **7. Data Retention**

Since we do not collect user data, there is no data retention policy. The only data stored on your device (last read chapter and theme) persists until:

- You uninstall the App
- You manually clear the App's data
- You restore your device to factory settings

---

## **8. Your Privacy Rights**

### **8.1 Right to Access**
You have the right to know what data is stored on your device. You can view the App's stored data through your device settings.

### **8.2 Right to Delete**
You have the right to delete all data stored by the App at any time:

**Android → Settings → Apps → Telugu Chronological Bible → Storage → Clear Data**

Or simply uninstall the App.

### **8.3 Right to Portability**
Your stored data (last read chapter and theme) is stored locally on your device in standard formats. You can export or backup this data using Android's backup features.

### **8.4 Right to Object**
Since we collect no data, there is nothing to object to. However, if you have concerns about how your device stores App data, you can uninstall the App at any time.

---
